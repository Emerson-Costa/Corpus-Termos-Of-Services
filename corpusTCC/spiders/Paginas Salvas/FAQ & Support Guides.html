Privacy Policy

Summary
We DO NOT sell, share or otherwise divulge any personal information to any third parties, except where required by law.

We ONLY store identifiable information, such as a name or email address, in cases where you have volunteered to do so as part of an in-app service (eg. profile requests or feedback).  This information, along with an ip address and device model, is used for the sole purpose of providing in-app services such as polls, profile requests, update notifications and/or to protect our digital services from abuse.

Further Detail
We may collect and store device or connection-specific information such as your IP address, access timestamps and device model, when using our services for the purpose of providing application update notifications, storing the results of any "in-app" polls or profile request or feedback submissions you may make, and to facilitate a better user experience within our apps, in addition to any other details you may volunteer to provide us. Access to online image search features use DuckDuckGo's search service, their privacy policy maybe found at https://duckduckgo.com/privacy .  If you opt to use Google Inc.'s search service in-app, their privacy policy may be found at https://www.google.com/intl/en/privacy/privacy-policy.html. We track some website usage using StatCounter in order to improve our web services, their privacy policy may be found at https://statcounter.com/about/legal.

What We Do With This Information
The information that we collect is stored temporarily in order to provide extended application functionality, interactivity and to enforce our internal security policy. We do not sell or divulge any of this information to any third parties, except where required by law or to facilitate provision of these services. Once the information has served it's purpose, ie. when a poll has finished, we permanently delete the associated information from our servers.
