ABOUT US

"We're artists who just happen to love games."

Life seems complicated these days. We’re all busy pursuing happiness, yet how many of us ever reach that goal? Success, money, and business are at the top of our priority list, and yet, deep in our hearts, we’d prefer time, fun and love. So, what really makes us happy, and how do we find it?

We at Jambav believe that one can learn to find joy and laughter in small things like a three year old does everyday. And what do 3 year olds do everyday? They play games.

Happiness Hack: Play games.

Reality is broken and our game designers can fix it. Video games are fulfilling genuine human needs, drawing on positive psychology, cognitive science and sociology. Game designers across the world have hit on core truths about what makes us happy, and utilized these discoveries to astonishing effect in virtual environments. We Jambavans, also realized this truth and wanted to make people happy with our games.

Why Jambav though?

Jambavan is a character originating in Indian epic poetry who assists Prince Ram in his struggle against Ravana. When hopes were lost, he is the one who encourages and invigorates Hanuman to undertake the task of leaping the ocean. His motivation played a vital role in helping Prince Ram rescue his wife Sita.

We want to be Jambavans motivating people to find joy in life's simplest pleasures through playing games.

Who doesn't love games?

We all love to solve puzzles, find answers and succeed at games. That eureka moment you feel when you finish a puzzle/game cannot be expressed through mere words. They provide instant gratification when completed. So, we Jambavans want to motivate you, take your mind off the illogical things in life, broaden your minds, increase your creativity and provide a fun workout for your brain cells.

Games make sense, even when the rest of your life may not.

So, go to the App Store, download our fun and addictive games. Start with PUDI. Best of luck hacking happiness!
