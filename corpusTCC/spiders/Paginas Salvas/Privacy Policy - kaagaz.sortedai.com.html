Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATE: 30th Jun 2020

Thank you for choosing to use Kaagaz - Scanner & Document Storage App. Kaagaz is a product of Ordenado Labs Pvt Ltd, a private limited company registered in India that has created a set of productivity tools (Sorted AI, SendLater, Kaagaz).
