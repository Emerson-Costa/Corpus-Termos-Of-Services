USE OF COOKIES

The game as well as the related Internet pages uses so-called Cookies or Flash Cookies (Local Shares Object) at multiple points.
Cookies serve to make our offer more user-friendly and effective. Cookies are small text files placed on Your computer and stored by Your browser. By itself, cookies cannot be used to identify the user. Cookies do not cause harm to Your computer and do not contain viruses.
The game as well as the related Internet pages can be used without Cookies, if the web browser is set in such a manner that cookies are generally denied or You are notified if a cookie is received, thus allowing You to decide every time whether You would like to accept it or not. You can manually delete cookies that are already on Your computer at any time from the hard drive. Please note that usage of the game and the related Internet pages without cookies may only be possible with limitations.
