Privacy Policy

In this policy, "User Information" shall mean information entered by the user in the Pedometer application, such as age.
Furthermore, the Pedometer app does not acquire location information.

User information is managed and displayed on the user's device.
ITO Technologies, Inc. will never send user information to external locations from the user's device without the consent of the user.

The Pedometer app supports sharing, backup, and restoring functions through which the user can send user information to external locations if they so desire.
By intentionally choosing to transmit user information, the user is considered to have granted permission to make a transmission to an external location, and user information will then be sent to the desired location.
User information can be sent to the following locations in these cases.

  • The app designated by the user through the share function.
  • The Google Drive account designated by the user through the backup/restore functions.

The Pedometer app will gather and send crash data and statistical information regarding the usage conditions of the app to the developers.
This crash data and statistical information will be used to improve the quality of the app.

The Pedometer app makes use of a third party advertising service (Google). The advertising service provider sometimes makes use of a technology which functions similarly to cookies in order to display advertisements. Furthermore, there are some cases in which it collects other miscellaneous information as well. Please refer to the Google Ads privacy policy page for more information.

The advertising service provider displays personalized advertisements (advertisements thought to be of high relevance to the user). It is possible to opt out of these personalized advertisements. Please refer to the Google Ads privacy policy page for more information.
