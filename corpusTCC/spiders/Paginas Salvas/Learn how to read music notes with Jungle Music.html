Jungle Music

Learning music becomes child's play!

Jungle Music is an educational game that lets you easily learn to read music notes in all clefs through an innovating, progressive, playful and instructive method, specially designed for beginner musicians and children age 5 and up.

Achieve your quest through the jungle of a desert island and feed your chameleon with flies-notes. The more you progress, the more he grows ... until he becomes the King of Music!

Download



Features

Each clef is divided into 20 levels of difficulty, allowing you to learn music notes at your own pace.
Earn stars by recognizing as many notes as possible.
Obtaining each level 3rd star is timed and unlocks the next level.


Jungle Music will improve your skills in :
✔ Score sight reading
✔ Ear training through listening to the sound of the note
✔ If a mistake is made, the correction is provided


An invaluable tool for learning music theory and solfege:
✔ Learn music notes in : G-Clef : Treble / F-Clefs : Bass, Baritone / C-Clefs : Soprano, Mezzo-Soprano, Alto, Tenor
✔ Reading on the staff, as well as reading under and above the staff
✔ Choose the writing of music notes among (Do Re Mi Fa Sol La Si) / (C D E F G A B) / (C D E F G A H)
✔ keyboard displaying for learning piano
✔ Custom level : Design your training !

Privacy policy

Jungle Music doesn't store or collect any personal information.
The only data tracking is your progression in the game.

You can play Jungle Music offline, without any internet connection at all.

Permissions are:
android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE & android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE :
For saving and reading your progression (levels & stars) in the game

android.permission.DISABLE_KEYGUARD & android.permission.WAKE_LOCK :
For preventing your device from being put on standby while you're playing
