Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 26th, 2019

This Privacy Policy applies to how your data is collected in the Gandr app and what we do with it.

The What & How of Information We Collect

When you use the Gandr app, some information is automatically collected. This app uses Google Analytics to collect information about your behaviour in the app. We use this information to determine which features our users like and dislike, which ultimately helps us improve the experience for you - the user. We never share any of the aforementioned data any company, person or group.

Upon saving a collage with the Gandr app, your collage photo may be automatically uploaded to Zazzle (https://www.zazzle.com/) for the sole purpose of offering products that you might be interested in. Your collage photo will be used to generate realistic product previews, using your actual collage. Your collage photo is never shared when any company, person or group and is automatically deleted from Google Cloud Storage within 24 hours of creating it. We use Google Cloud Storage to host your collage image for Zazzle's service.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy that we think materially alter your rights, then we will post the latest policy to this site and change the "Last Updated" date above. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy whenever you use the Gandr app.
