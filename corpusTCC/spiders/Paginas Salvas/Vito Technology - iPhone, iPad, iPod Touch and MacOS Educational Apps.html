A. COLLECTED INFORMATION

Information We Collect

As a visitor to our Services, you can engage in many activities without providing any Personal information. In the sense of GDPR “Personal information” means any information that identifies directly or indirectly a particular individual, such as individual’s name, postal address, e-mail, telephone number. In connection with other activities, such as subscribing to periodic newsletters sent by Vito Technology, requesting customer support, utilizing online software applications or purchasing any of our products, Vito Technology may ask you to voluntarily supply us with such Personal information as email address and your name. It is completely optional for you to engage in these activities and/or make any purchases.


Credit Card and other payment information are not retained by us as part of the registration process. This information is processed by our payment and billing providers during the purchase process and never disclosed to us.


As previously mentioned above you are provided an opportunity on our websites and within our products to subscribe to a periodic newsletter. This newsletter is a marketing tool that allows Vito Technology to inform you of new products or services, as well as other news relevant to the company. The subscription service requires you to provide your email address only.


By creating Star Walk® account in our Services, you agree to sending of the newsletters to your email address provided by you in the registration process.


When requesting customer support you will be required to provide us with your email address in order to send you reply and optionally the name.


In addition, we may use the information made publicly available, e.g. through YouTube, vk.com, Facebook, Instagram and Twitter, in order to find out your opinion about our Services, so that we could take them into account while improving such Services.


We may collect, process and share with third parties (as further specified in Section How Your Information Is Shared below) certain non-personal technical information about your computer or mobile device, such as IP number, browser information, OS version, identifiers associated with the Device (as this term is defined below), location, connections, network type and other. Such technical information does not identify a specific person.


We may collect, process and share with third parties (as further specified in Section How Your Information Is Shared below) the aggregated, anonymized information received via our Services that do not identify any single user.


Online Tracking Technologies


When you use the Services, we or third parties authorized by us, such as Facebook, Google that provide content, advertising, or other functionality within the Services, may use cookies, pixel tags, local storage, mobile device identifiers, APIs, SDKs and other technologies (“Technologies”) to automatically collect information through the Services. Such Technologies that are essentially small data files placed on your computer, tablet, mobile phone, or other devices (referred to collectively as a “Device”), allow us and authorized third parties to record certain pieces of information whenever you visit or interact with the Services, to recognize, identify you across Devices and when you sign into the Services, to serve ads that may be relevant to your interests, to understand the usefulness to you of such ads, to measure advertising effectiveness, to allow more effective targeted advertising, to enhance your experience with Services, to improve the quality of our Services, to tailor recommendations to your interests and make the Services easier to use.

We may collect and use your Personal information for the following purposes: to provide you with the Services you request, to uniquely identify you as a user in our Services, deliver newsletters, security alerts, administrative messages to you based on your activity and location data, provide technical support, including product updates, to registry you as a participant in any contest, to arrange receipt of the prize to which you may be entitled, carry out other purposes that are disclosed to you and to which you consent.


We may use the geo-location data to surface the appropriate country/language-based version of our Services to you.


We analyze non-personal, technical, statistical information to define how to improve the Services and our operations to make them more interesting for our users in general.


How Your Information Is Shared


We may share your Personal information with our parent, subsidiary and other affiliated companies (each an "Affiliate") and as described below. Our Affiliates will follow privacy practices no less protective for all users than our practices described in this document, to the extent allowed by applicable law. We prohibit the sale or transfer of Personal information to entities outside of the Vito Technology's Affiliates for their use without your approval.


We may disclose the Personal information we collect to our business partners who commit to comply with the applicable laws relating their usage of the information subject to receipt of your advance respective consent. These parties are contractually prohibited from processing Personal information for any purpose other than for performing legitimate contractual obligations or to comply with applicable legal requirements.


We do provide non-personal, technical, statistical information to certain service providers and advertising partners for their use on an aggregated, anonymized basis for the purpose of performing their contractual obligations to us. In particular, this enables our advertisers to develop and deliver targeted advertising in the Services and on the websites of third parties, so that they can try to serve you with advertisements for products and services that are most likely to be of interest to you. Advertisers will also use this information to monitor, improve or modify their operations.


Although we will always strive to safeguard your private information, there may be circumstances where we are forced to disclose this information without your permission. We will only do if we have a good-faith belief that disclosure is reasonably necessary to (1) comply with laws, regulations, or government requests or (2) to investigate or protect against harmful activities to you or other users.

It is important for you to know that Personal information you submit through our Services may be transferred across national boundaries and may be stored and processed in any of the countries in which we maintain our offices or web hosting servers. As a result, how this information is collected or used is governed the by the laws of those countries.

These third parties may use cookies and/or web beacons to identify your preferences or to recognize if you have previously had contact them. If you have any concerns about these companies or their content, please direct them to the administrator or webmaster for the company in question.
