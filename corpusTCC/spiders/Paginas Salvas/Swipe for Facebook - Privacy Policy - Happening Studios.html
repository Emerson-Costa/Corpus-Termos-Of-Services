Swipe for Facebook – Privacy Policy

 

What personal information do we collect?

We do not collect any information whatsoever, zilch. All account details and information entered goes directly to Facebook’s servers, and we do not intercept any connections in any way, shape, or form.

 

The only information we collect – Crash Logs

The only information we collect, which is of value to us, are crash reports. This is done through Google’s Firebase Crashlytics, as well as the Play Store’s own built-in crash reporting. No associated account information (whether Facebook or Google account data) is included in these reports.

 

Why do we collect Crash Logs?

Simple – to help us fix bugs and crashes.
