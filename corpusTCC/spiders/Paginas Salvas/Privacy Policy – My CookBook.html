Privacy Policy

The ‘My CookBook’ Android application is provided by the MAADINFO SERVICES Company.

The ‘My CookBook’ application can be used with an optional service offered by the website My CookBook Online : http://www.mycookbook-online.net
This service allows users to synchronize their recipes with their online account.

By using the application ‘My CookBook’ with the ‘My CookBook Online’ Service, MAADINFO SERVICES may collect and process your personal data. MAADINFO SERVICES is committed to ensuring that your private information is protected. Please check the My CookBook Online privacy policy for more details : http://www.mycookbook-online.net/en/privacy/

By using the application ‘My CookBook’ without the ‘My CookBook Online’ Service, MAADINFO SERVICES does not collect and process any personnal data.

MAADINFO SERVICES may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are happy with any changes.

Non personnal collected data

Third-party collected data : We use several third parties in order to improve our services. These parties collect their own data. Please read their privacy policies to ensure that you’re comfortable with the manner in which they collect data.

Third parties:

Google Analytics: Application usage information is anonymously tracked using Google Analytics for Android, in accordance with their privacy policy, to allow us to determine which features are most popular, how they are being used, and how we can improve the applications. No personally identifying data is included in this type of reporting.
My CookBook has implemented Google Analytics Display Advertising Features including Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting.
You can opt-out of the Google Analytics Display Advertiser Features through Ads Settings, Ad Settings for mobile apps or this add-on https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/

Google Admob: The free version of our android application includes some ads displayed with Google Admob https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/

Google custom search: The search of new recipes is provided by Google Inc. You acknowledge and agree to respect the confidentiality rules of Google (available at http://www.google.fr/privacy.html) through the use of the research box. By using the search box, you agree that Google uses your personal data according to rules of confidentiality.

Facebook : You can use Facebook buttons if you want to like our website or share a recipe. These buttons function using code provided by Facebook respectively. Please read the privacy declarations of Facebook respectively (which can change regularly) to determine what they do with your personal data that they process via these cookies.

Dropbox : You can backup your recipes or shopping lists on your dropbox account, or import data from dropbox. Please check their policy if you want to use that feature : https://www.dropbox.com/privacy

External Links : When searching a new recipe, you may open some external Web sites. This Web sites may collect their own data. This Policy does not address, and we are not responsible for, the privacy practices of Web sites operated by third parties, whether they are linked to or otherwise accessible from the application. The inclusion of a link or accessibility of third party Web sites does not imply endorsement of such third party Web site by us.

Use of collected information

These data and services are used to ensure the proper functioning of the application and to improve the quality of our services.

Data stored

By using the application ‘My CookBook’ without the ‘My CookBook Online’ Service, MAADINFO SERVICES does not store any data on their servers.
