2. Collection and use

PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION (PII)

Evrika Games collects personally identifiable information (“PII”) when you choose to provide it to us. PII is any information that specifically identifies or locates a particular person or entity. We receive and store certain types of PII. The types of PII collected may include:

  • your ID in social media
  • IP address
  • your email when you contact us via email

Evrika Games uses this information for reasonable business purposes, including to contact you to respond to your inquiry, to provide the service requested. We may share Client PII with partners as necessary to improve the Service. Once obtained, Evrika Games will store your PII for as long as needed to provide services and for a reasonable time thereafter for record keeping purposes.

Evrika Games may collect, record and analyze information of Users to its Products. Evrika Games may add information collected by way of pageview activity. If you provide Evrika Games with your social media details, Evrika Games may retrieve publicly available information about you from social media. We DO NOT collect, store or operate any information about: age, gender, political thoughts, religious beliefs, phone number etc, even if User would like to share such information with us directly.

NON-PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION

Evrika Games also collects non-personally identifiable information (“Non-PII”) from our Products. Non-PII is pseudonymous information that cannot by itself be used to specifically identify a particular person or entity, and may include your purchases, completed tasks or quests, your in-product achievement, ranking score, length of time spent using Products, actions performed within our Products, statistics, smartphone type and model, screen resolution and ID, usage habits, the time/date of your visit to the Products and your device’s operating system and its version. We may use a third-party analytics provider to help us collect and manage this data (check out our third party services). We use this information to help manage and improve the Products.

By using our software, you consent to the collection and use of your non-personally identifiable information.

If you don’t want us to keep and process such information, please contact us directly to delete any of your records, accounts and all the data we collected about you. You may not use our products after that. You cannot reverse this action and no refunds would be applicable.
