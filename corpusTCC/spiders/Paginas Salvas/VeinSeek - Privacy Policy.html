VeinSeek Pro and VeinSeek Android request access to your smartphone camera to enable image processing to highlight veins; without this access, there would be no app! At no time do any of our applications transmit or otherwise use the data captured by your smartphone. All your camera's sensor data stays where it belongs: on your smartphone, not accessed by us.
