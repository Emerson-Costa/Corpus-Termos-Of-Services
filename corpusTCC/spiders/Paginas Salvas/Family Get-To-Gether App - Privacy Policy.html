General
FamilyGTG app does not collect any information about you. The app also does not collect any information about GEDCOM files you import.
Documents Import Report
The documents import report is disabled by default. In case of being enabled explicitly by you, the app wraps up a report about the photo files being referenced in the GEDCOM file and whether they are imported successfully or not. The report contains some information which includes:
- Photo file names.
- GEDCOM file path name on your device.
- Individual record IDs for members (no names) whose referenced photo files.
The report is displayed and it is only sent to us via email if "Send Report" option is selected.
Facebook Login
The Facebook login action is optional. In case you have explicitly logged-in Facebook, the app can facilitate the process of importing family members. The following Facebook family members data is used: First & last name, and small & large photo. Also, gender info is sometimes used when available. The following Facebook permissions are used.
- public_profile: Your public profile basic information. First & last names, small & large photo and gender information is used.
- user_friends: Your Facebook friends who also accepted one or more permissions of this app. First & last names and small & large photo information is used.
- user_relationships: Your Facebook family members list. First & last names, small & large photo, and relation (brother, sister, ...) information is used.
Please contact us if you have any queries about the privacy policy.
