Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

We only collect an advertising identifier that is unique to your device. This is required for us to be able to display advertisements in our apps.

What do we use your information for?

Before collecting any information we always ask for your consent. The ad identifier is used by our advertising partners to show you non-personalized ads. The companies linked below are being used to provide you with ads, in order to find out more information on how they handle the data please visit their respective privacy policy:

Admob (owned by Google)Â – https://support.google.com/admob/answer/7665968

Unity – https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

Why do we collect any data?

We only collect the data that is absolutely necessary for us to display advertisements. The revenue generated from advertisements is our only income.

Why do we require certain Permissions in our apps?

(1) INTERNET: This permission is required to display ads. We do not collect any personal data.

(2) ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: See (1)

(3) WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: This permission is only used for saving and loading images which the user has painted him/herself. This is entirely optional.

(4) READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: See (3)

(4) MICROPHONE: This permission is used to record audio from the microphone. The recorded data is NOT saved on the device. It is only cached in memory for a short while (approximatelly 3 seconds) and used for distorting the recorded audio to a higher pitch making it a fun tool.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

No.
