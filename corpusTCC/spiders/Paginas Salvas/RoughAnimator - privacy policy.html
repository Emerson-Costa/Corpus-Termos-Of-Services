Privacy policy

I cannot and do not collect any personal information through the RoughAnimator software itself.

Apple, Google, and Amazon may collect information you give them in order to pay for the software, which I have no access to. They may also collect anonymous usage data such as crash logs, which I do have access to and use to improve the software, but have no way of knowing who that data came from. Their privacy policies can be found here:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
https://policies.google.com/privacy
https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=468496

If you buy the software for MacOS or Windows, that is done through FastSpring. The name, email, and address that you enter during checkout will be kept in FastSpring's system and I can access this for statistical purposes, to keep your licenses, and in order to contact you by email, if there's a need. I do not have access to any of your payment information at any point. FastSpring's privacy policy can be found here:
https://fastspring.com/privacy/

If you email me, I will save your emails for purposes of responding to them and telling you relevant information for support.

You can email me to ask me to delete any information I keep about you. This includes personally identifiable sales information and any past email discussions.

I may update this privacy information as needed. The latest version will always appear on this page. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me.
